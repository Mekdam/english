Consumer Awareness

Protecting the integrity of marketing. Be wary of these common tactics:

  • "You've Won!" Scams: Real companies don't ask for fees to claim prizes. If you have to pay to get it, it's not a prize.
  • Fake Giveaways: Verify the social media profile's authenticity first. Look for verification badges and account history.
  • Phishing: Never click links in unsolicited texts or emails. These often lead to fake login pages.
  • Urgency Tactics: Scammers create a false sense of urgency, claiming an offer expires in minutes to stop you from thinking clearly.
  • Unusual Payment Requests: Be suspicious if asked to pay via gift cards, cryptocurrency, or wire transfers.
